Reliability User Story
=======================

.. story:: Appliance Reliability - Just Works on Power
   :id: US_RELIABLE_1
   :status: approved
   :links: US_SETUP_1, REQ_SYS_HW_1, REQ_SYS_REL_1, REQ_SYS_PERF_1, REQ_STARTUP_1, REQ_STARTUP_2, REQ_STARTUP_3
   :tags: reliability, startup, garage-user

   **Role:** Garage user

   **Story:**
   As a garage user, I want the device to start working as soon as I plug it in
   after initial setup, so that I never have to think about it — it behaves like
   an appliance.

   **Value:**
   No button to press, no app to open, no reconfiguration after a power cut.
   Once configured, the device boots, runs its startup checks, and is ready before
   the garage door has finished opening.
