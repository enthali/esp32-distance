   **Role:** Garage user

   **Story:**
   As a garage user, I want to see how far my car is from the wall on a LED strip,
   so that I can park at the right spot without guessing.

   **Value:**
   The LED strip gives immediate, glanceable feedback during parking — no smartphone,
   no app, no thinking required. Green means go, the position shows how close you are,
   red means stop.
